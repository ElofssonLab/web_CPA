1. Summary


The Pconsfold2 pipeline uses the contact predictions from PconsC3, the CONFOLD folding algorithm and model quality estimations to predict structure of proteins. Here we show the result of applying this to Pfam families, both with and without known models.



2. Usage


The main site shows a list of all Pfam families and their generated models. Click on a family of interest to see details.
